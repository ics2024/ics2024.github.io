Registration

The registration site will open in April.

Visa Information

Citizens of certain countries are required to obtain a visa to enter Japan. Please visit the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Japan (MOFA) website to determine if you need to obtain a visa and to understand the visa application procedures: MOFA Visa Information.

Important Notice for Visa Support

  • Visa support is provided exclusively for individuals with a specific interest in our event.

    • This includes speakers (main track and workshops), poster presenters, exhibitors, committee members (Program Committee, Organizing Committee, Workshop Committees), researchers actively contributing to the HPC community, and their affiliated students. Inquiries from companies and agencies will not be addressed.

  • Visa support will only be offered after the completion of the registration payment.

    • For visa support requests or related inquiries, please contact visa_request_at_arch.cs.titech.ac.jp (replace '_at_' with @) with your paper info (the title of your accepted paper) or the registration details and proof of payment. Ensure to include "ICS2024 Visa Support Request" in the subject line of your email to facilitate prompt processing.
