ICS 2024: International Conference on Supercomputing

June 4-7, 2024

International Exchange Hall, Kyoto University, Kyoto, Japan

ACM International Conference on Supercomputing (ICS) is the premier international forum for the presentation of research results in high-performance computing systems.

The 38th conference (ICS 2024) will be held in Kyoto, Japan on June 4-7, 2024. And more importantly, this year's conference is a STAND-ALONE EVENT, which is NOT part of the Federated Computing Research Conference.

ICS 2024 WILL BE HELD IN-PERSON STYLE. We will NOT support real-time streaming of the conference to many people or take questions remotely, so please make a registration and come to the venue. Also, if you plan to make a presentation at the workshop or conference, please DO NOT "No Show". If you do, you know the consequences.

We look forward to seeing you in Kyoto!
